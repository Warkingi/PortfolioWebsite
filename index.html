About Me

It has always been a passion of mine to create engaging worlds through game design and storytelling that leave a lasting impression on the people who experience them. From a young age, I found myself building games for friends and family to enjoy while driven by the excitement of watching them play.

I’ve always believed designing the best games starts with understanding the player. By playing alongside the people who bring these experiences to life, I can better understand what makes a game become a lasting memory rather than something easily forgotten.

Projects

Empires: Red vs Blue

Game Designer Level Designer Verse Developer
2023-2025

Developed during the early days of Fortnite Creative 2.0, Empires: Red vs Blue was my most prized UEFN project. Originally Empires was developed under the name of Twilight Hollow and featured 4 players independently fighting for control over the grove. However, adapting to the UEFN Ecosystem was necessary and Twilight Hollow's gameplay had its issues.

Listening to feedback and getting the chance to engage with players personally, I developed a new streamlined 2.0 update and renamed the project to Empires.

Features

  • Fully customisable player loadouts.
  • Leveling system for individual weapons.
  • Unique perks for individual gameplay.
  • Earn gold and purchase items at NPC vendors.
  • Large detailed map with 7 PoIs.
  • Warfare on an epic scale.

Graveyard RPG

Game Designer Verse Developer
2025

Graveyard was originally developed as a seasonal project but eventually pivoted into an RPG. This was an ambitious project with one thing in mind: how far can we push UEFN when trying to create an MMORPG? This was no easy task as UEFN was not yet built with the tools necessary to create an MMORPG, requiring outside the box thinking when designing features.

Developed in only a month, Graveyard comes fully equipped with a custom inventory vault, hostile RPG-style creatures, quests, world pvp, unlockable powerup system, party grouping and a single dungeon with a custom boss fight at the end. This was a remarkable feat and pushed the limits of UEFN.

Features

  • RPG Leveling system with stat scaling.
  • Class powers allowing for deeper customisation.
  • Loot vault for storing all player unlocks.
  • Dungeon system allowing for both solo and co-op.
  • PvE and optional PvP flagging.

World of Heroes

Game Designer Level Designer Datapack Developer Narrative Director
2023-2024

World of Heroes is a passion project that remains unreleased to the public and was developed out of my love for MMORPGs, more specifically Warcraft.

Developed using Minecraft datapacks, World of Heroes is an ambitious project that pushes the limits of what is possible without mods. The world is handcrafted and based on a story I've been working on since the early 2010's. Complete with a vast world full of quests, loot and danger, it truly is a labour of love.

Features

  • Full handcrafted open world ready for adventure.
  • RPG Leveling system with stat scaling.
  • Indepth questing system with roleplaying elements.
  • Class masteries allowing for different playstyles.
  • Limited time events.
  • Dungeon system with custom bosses and loot.
  • Challenging arena system with custom bosses.
  • Professions and craftable items.
